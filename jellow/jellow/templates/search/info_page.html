"The power of the people is stronger than the people in power."

Jellow is a peer-to-peer news stream that allows users and readers to discover and share the latest news and ideas on world issues that are currently occurring. All news topics are updated ‘live’, allowing readers and users to communicate and dissect the “hot” topics with one another directly by the use of hashtags, searches and rubric grading. We want to bring you the real world through real people.

Join us in the digital revolution of the news media by writing an article of a current affair that pertains to you. Read through others' articles and grade them on the Jellow rubric so you can keep the community updated on the most reliable information. Please also note that we are constantly striving to make the system more effective in order to bring you the most resourceful news content; so keep us informed about any suggestions or new features you would like to see.
